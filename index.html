Install the Extension

Click the "Install Extension" button and add OTS to your Chrome browser. It takes just seconds!

2

Open the Extension

Click the OTS icon in your Chrome toolbar to open the extension popup.

3

Add Your Data

Click the + button to add key-value pairs. For example: Name ‚Üí John Doe, Email ‚Üí john@example.com

4

When you encounter a form, open the extension and click the Fill button next to each data item to populate matching fields.
