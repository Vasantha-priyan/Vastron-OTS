Privacy Policy for Form Auto-Filler Extension

Last Updated: December 16, 2025

Overview

Form Auto-Filler ("we," "our," or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our Chrome extension collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Form Field Data: When you save form values using our extension, this data is stored locally in your browser
  • Field Labels: Custom names you assign to saved form values

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not track your browsing activity
  • We do not collect analytics or usage statistics
  • We do not access your browsing history
  • We do not collect any data from websites you visit

How We Use Your Information

Local Storage Only

  • All saved form data is stored exclusively in Chrome's local sync storage
  • Data syncs across your Chrome devices through Google's built-in sync service
  • We never access, transmit, or store your data on external servers

Extension Functionality

Your saved data is used solely to:

  • Display your saved form values in the extension popup
  • Fill form fields when you use the FILL button
  • Enable drag-and-drop functionality to form fields
  • Copy values to your clipboard when requested

Data Sharing and Third Parties

No Data Sharing

  • We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your data with any third parties
  • We do not have access to your data
  • Your data never leaves your browser except through Chrome's built-in sync

Chrome Sync Service

  • If you have Chrome sync enabled, your saved form data will sync across your Chrome devices
  • This is handled entirely by Google's Chrome sync service
  • We do not control or have access to this sync process

Data Security

Local Storage Security

  • All data is stored using Chrome's secure storage APIs
  • Data is encrypted as part of Chrome's standard security measures
  • Only you have access to your saved form data

No External Transmission

  • Your data is never transmitted to external servers
  • No network requests are made by our extension
  • All processing happens locally in your browser

Your Rights and Controls

Data Control

  • You can view all saved data in the extension popup
  • You can delete individual form values at any time
  • You can uninstall the extension to remove all data
  • Chrome sync settings control cross-device synchronization

Permissions Explanation

Why We Need These Permissions

Storage Permission

  • Required to save your form field data locally
  • Enables sync across your Chrome devices
  • No external storage is used

Active Tab Permission

  • Needed to fill form fields on the current webpage
  • Only accesses the tab when you actively use the fill feature
  • No background monitoring or data collection

Scripting Permission

  • Required for drag-and-drop functionality
  • Enables automatic form filling
  • Scripts only run when you interact with the extension

Host Permissions (All URLs)

  • Allows the extension to work on any website
  • Only activates when you use the fill or drag-and-drop features
  • No automatic data collection from websites

Children's Privacy

Our extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The extension is designed for general productivity use and does not target children specifically.

Data Retention

  • No automatic data expiration or deletion occurs
  • You have complete control over data retention
